How to Reset Your Password

Follow these simple steps to reset your MEOD account password

1

Go to the Login Page

Navigate to the MEOD login page at www.meod.com/login or click the "Login" button on the homepage.

2

Click "Forgot Password?"

Below the password field, you'll see a "Forgot Password?" link. Click on it to begin the password reset process.

3

Enter Your Email Address

Type in the email address associated with your MEOD account. Make sure it's the same email you used when you registered.

4

Check Your Email

We'll send you a password reset link to your email address. The email should arrive within 5 minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it in your inbox.

5

Click the Reset Link

Open the email and click on the "Reset Password" button or link. This will take you to a secure page where you can create a new password.

6

Create a New Password

Enter your new password twice to confirm it. Your password should be at least 8 characters long and include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols for better security.

7

Log In with Your New Password

Once your password is reset, you'll be redirected to the login page. Use your email and new password to access your account.
